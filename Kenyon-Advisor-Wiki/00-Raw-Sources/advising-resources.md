We were the first college in the country to introduce the model of professors serving as academic advisors. Today, this celebrated practice continues to be a distinguishing feature of a Kenyon education.
In addition to working one-on-one with your faculty advisor, you’ll find numerous resources — from academic advising to peer mentoring — to help you get the advice and support you need along the way.
### Office of Academic Advising
From year one to four, the [Office of Academic Advising](https://www.kenyon.edu/academics/advising-resources/office-of-academic-advising/) works with you to identify your strengths and weaknesses, and helps you explore opportunities to reach your professional goals.
### Career Development Office
The [Career Development Office](https://www.kenyon.edu/careers-outcomes/career-development-office/) is your go-to resource to learn about professional opportunities, pursue internships, network with alumni and develop a strategy for your career search.
### Student Accessibility and Support Services
If you have a disability or temporary medical condition, [Student Accessibility and Support Services](https://www.kenyon.edu/offices-and-services/student-accessibility-and-support-services/) (SASS) can coordinate and develop customized and confidential accommodation plans to make the most of your learning experience.

## We're in this together.
At Kenyon, we all learn from one another. You’ll find valuable advice and guidance from your peers in these programs:
### Math and Science Skills Center 
Peer tutors at [MSSC](https://www.kenyon.edu/academics/advising-resources/math-and-science-skills-center/) are ready to assist you with homework, lab reports or conceptual questions in a collaborative, interactive environment.
### Writing Center
Looking for ways to combat writer’s block? Need help finding a new angle on your research paper? Tutors at the [Writing Center](https://www.kenyon.edu/academics/advising-resources/writing-center/) can lend an ear and a hand.
### REACH Peer Mentoring
If you’re an underrepresented or first-generation student, [REACH](https://www.kenyon.edu/campus-life/inclusion-belonging/programs-services/) peer mentors offer guidance and support as you navigate the college experience. 
### STEM Peer Mentoring
If science is your passion, the [K-STEM Peer Mentoring program](https://www.kenyon.edu/academics/stem-scholars-program/k-stem-program-peer-mentoring/) can help you explore and discover opportunities in STEM fields.
